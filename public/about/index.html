Developer 🥑 Advocate

Amazon Web Services

Seb has been writing code since he first laid his hands on a Commodore 64 in the mid‑eighties. Since then, he has watched programming languages evolve, architectures transform, and the cloud redefine how modern systems are built. Today, he is a Developer Advocate at Amazon Web Services, helping builders get the most out of the cloud, with a constant obsession: performance, simplicity, and technical elegance. His favorite playgrounds? Distributed architectures, developer tools, mobile computing… and everything that enables teams to ship faster, safer, and at scale.

A regular speaker at AWS Summit, a blogger on the AWS News Blog (aka “Jeff’s blog”), host of the official AWS 🇫🇷 Podcast, and contributor to the Belgian tech podcast Les Technos, Sébastien enjoys explaining complex topics with concrete examples and code that compiles. Before that, he led the AWS Technical Training teams across EMEA and the Alexa Solution Architecture teams in Southern Europe.

His driving forces: curiosity, knowledge sharing, and one simple conviction — if it cannot be automated through an API, there is probably still work to do.
